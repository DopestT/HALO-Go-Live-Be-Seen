# App Store Description

## App Name
HALO — Go Live, Be Seen

## Subtitle (30 characters max)
Premium Live Streaming

## Description (4000 characters max)

**Go live and be seen in your most authentic moments.**

HALO is a premium live streaming platform designed for genuine connection and artistic expression. Our dark-first aesthetic with glassmorphism design creates a calm, beautiful space where presence matters more than performance.

**KEY FEATURES**

**Live Streaming**
• Broadcast high-quality live video to your audience
• Real-time interaction with viewers through chat
• Seamless streaming experience with low latency
• Professional-grade audio and video quality

**Discovery**
• Explore trending streams in a low-density, comfortable feed
• Find creators based on your interests
• Generous spacing and clean design for easy browsing
• No overwhelming notifications or urgency-based prompts

**Safety First**
• One-tap reporting system for immediate action
• Block users with a single tap
• Age-verified adult mode for 18+ content
• Moderated community with clear guidelines
• Your safety is our priority

**Beautiful Design**
• Dark-first aesthetic with Void Black background
• Glassmorphism effects for modern, premium feel
• Rounded geometry throughout (no sharp edges)
• Tabular numbers for clean data display
• No neon colors or gaming-style elements

**Authentic Experience**
• Calm, respectful language throughout
• No pressure tactics or urgency-based copy
• Focus on presence over performance metrics
• Community-first approach to social networking

**Privacy & Security**
• End-to-end encrypted streams
• No data selling or invasive tracking
• Clear privacy controls
• Transparent about data usage

**WHO IS HALO FOR?**

HALO is perfect for:
• Creators who value authenticity over virality
• Artists sharing their creative process
• Musicians performing live sessions
• Educators hosting interactive lessons
• Communities building genuine connections
• Anyone who appreciates beautiful, thoughtful design

**ADULT MODE**

For users 18 and older, Adult Mode provides access to age-appropriate content with additional age verification. All adult content is clearly labeled and requires explicit opt-in.

**SAFETY & MODERATION**

We take safety seriously:
• 24/7 moderation team
• AI-assisted content filtering
• One-tap reporting for streams, messages, and users
• Immediate blocking capabilities
• Clear community guidelines
• Age verification for adult content
• Regular safety audits

**DESIGN PHILOSOPHY**

HALO was built on the principle that social platforms should be calm, respectful spaces that enhance rather than drain your energy. Our design choices reflect this:

• **Presence over Performance**: We don't show view counts during live streams to reduce anxiety
• **Quality over Quantity**: Low-density feeds give each stream breathing room
• **Calm over Chaos**: No gaming HUDs, neon colors, or overwhelming interfaces
• **Respect over Urgency**: No pressure tactics or FOMO-inducing language
