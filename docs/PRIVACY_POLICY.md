# Privacy Policy - HALO

**Last Updated**: February 13, 2026

## Introduction

HALO ("we", "us", or "our") operates the HALO mobile application (the "Service"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
When you create an account, we collect:
- Email address
- Username
- Profile picture (optional)
- Date of birth (for age verification)
- Display name

### Usage Data
We automatically collect:
- Device information (type, operating system, unique device identifiers)
- Log data (IP address, browser type, pages visited, time spent)
- Stream metadata (stream duration, viewer count, engagement metrics)

### Content Data
When you use our streaming features:
- Live video streams (not permanently stored unless you choose to save)
- Chat messages
- User-generated content (profile information, stream titles, descriptions)

## How We Use Your Information

We use collected data for:
- **Providing the Service**: Account creation, authentication, streaming functionality
- **Improving the Service**: Analytics, bug fixes, feature development
- **Safety & Moderation**: Enforcing community guidelines, investigating reports
- **Communication**: Service updates, security alerts, support responses
- **Legal Compliance**: Complying with legal obligations

## Data Storage & Security

### Storage
- User data is stored on secure servers
- Live streams are transmitted in real-time and not stored unless explicitly saved by the creator
- Chat messages are stored for moderation purposes and deleted after 90 days

### Security Measures
- End-to-end encryption for live streams
- Secure password hashing (bcrypt)
- JWT-based authentication
- Regular security audits
- HTTPS/TLS for all data transmission

## Data Sharing

We **DO NOT** sell your personal data. We may share data only in these circumstances:

### Service Providers
We use third-party services for:
- Cloud hosting (AWS)
- Analytics (privacy-focused analytics only)
- Payment processing (for future premium features)

### Legal Requirements
We may disclose data when required by law or to:
- Comply with legal processes
- Protect rights, property, or safety
- Investigate potential violations
- Prevent fraud or security issues

### Public Information
Information you choose to make public:
- Your profile (username, display name, profile picture)
- Public streams and their metadata
- Public comments and interactions

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

### Access
Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you

### Correction
Update or correct your personal information at any time

### Deletion
Request deletion of your account and associated data
- Some data may be retained for legal/safety reasons
- Anonymized analytics data may be retained

### Opt-Out
- Marketing communications (we don't send marketing emails by default)
- Non-essential data collection
- Adult content (via app settings)

### Data Portability
Request your data in a machine-readable format

## Age Requirements

### General Use
- Must be 13 years or older to create an account
- Users under 18 require parental consent

### Adult Content
- Must be 18 years or older to access Adult Mode
- Age verification required
- Failure to verify age results in restricted access

## Cookies & Tracking

We use minimal tracking:
- **Essential Cookies**: Required for app functionality (authentication, session management)
- **Analytics**: Privacy-focused analytics to improve the service
- **No Third-Party Advertising Cookies**: We do not use advertising trackers

## Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If we discover we have collected data from a child under 13, we will delete it immediately. If you believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at privacy@haloapp.live.

## International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and maintained on servers located outside your country. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international transfers.

## Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as necessary:
- **Account Data**: Until you delete your account, plus 30 days
- **Chat Messages**: 90 days from creation
- **Stream Metadata**: As long as your account is active
- **Legal Compliance**: As required by law (typically 7 years)

## Third-Party Services

Our app may contain links to third-party services. We are not responsible for their privacy practices. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

### Third-Party Services We Use:
- LiveKit (for real-time streaming infrastructure)
- AWS (for cloud hosting)
- Expo (for app development and deployment)

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

## Your California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights:
- Right to know what personal information is collected
- Right to know if personal information is sold or disclosed
- Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information (we don't sell data)
- Right to deletion
- Right to non-discrimination for exercising your rights

## GDPR Compliance (EU Users)

If you are in the European Union, you have rights under GDPR:
- **Lawful Basis**: We process data based on consent, contract, or legitimate interests
- **Data Protection Officer**: Available upon request
- **Right to Lodge Complaint**: With your local supervisory authority
